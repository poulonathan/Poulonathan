Pregcare is an IoT wearable device that helps Health workers remotely monitor health vitals of Pregnant mothers in real time

Pregcare Device is able to track the following key vitals from the Pregnant mothers for Monitoring

  • Real Time Body Temperature
  • Reat Tim Heart Beat Rate
  • Real Time Pressure
  • Real Time GPS
  • Real Conversation with Mothers
